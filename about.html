Prodigy Dashboard

Easily hack your prodigy math game accounts online

Prodigy Dashboard is an online application used for making intergrations with prodigy’s API.

Features

Player hacks:

  • Update your player’s level.
  • Update your player’s gold.
  • Update your player’s first, middle and last name.
  • Update your player’s nickname.

Currency hacks:

  • Manage all the currencies your player have in the game.

Pet hacks:

  • Edit your pre-existing pet’s level.
  • Add new pets with custom level to your player’s pet inventory.
  • Get all the pets in the game with a custom level.

Other hacks:

  • Update your current dark tower floor level.
  • Update your player’s bounty points amount.
  • Update your account’s grade.

Usage

There’s two options for using this application.

Fast method:

Visit our published copy of this application here.

Slow method:

  1. Visit the applications source here.
  2. Click the green button labeled “Code”.
  3. Click on “Download ZIP”.
  4. Extract the file using an external service like 7-Zip.
  5. Open index.html with a browser.

We are not trying in any way to ruin prodigy math game, we are actually trying to enhance their security. Prodigy has not listened to many efforts we have made to contact them about this matter.
Some may ask, why make this a public service then? Well, the answer to that is if we didn’t release this to the public, it would have absolutely no effect on prodigy and they would never recognize.
